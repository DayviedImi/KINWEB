You can enroll your child by visiting our Programs page and selecting the program that fits your child's age and interests. Each program has registration details and requirements. For specific questions, feel free to contact us using the form above.

We welcome volunteers who share our vision for raising godly children. Requirements include:

  • Commitment to our values and mission
  • Background check (for working with children)
  • Minimum 6-month commitment
  • Participation in orientation training

Your donations directly support our programs and initiatives:

  • Educational materials and resources for children
  • Scholarships for the Psalm 119 Challenge
  • Facility rentals for events and meetings
  • Technology for virtual programs
  • Staff and volunteer training

We are committed to financial transparency and accountability.

We love partnering with churches to reach more children. Partnership opportunities include:

  • Hosting KidsInspiring programs at your church
  • Sponsoring children from your community to attend
  • Organizing joint events and outreaches
  • Sharing our vision with your congregation

Contact us using the form above to discuss partnership possibilities.
