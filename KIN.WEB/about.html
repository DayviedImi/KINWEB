Discovering Our Core Values

KidsInspiring Nation began as a divine revelation - a heart cry for the kingdom and our nation, Nigeria. During a soul-winning drive with Living Faith Church, the Holy Spirit spoke clearly: "The soul of a child is as important as the soul of an adult."

What started in 2017 as an after-Sunday school gathering has blossomed into a global movement. We discovered that raising children as gods (John 10:35) requires focusing on three core areas: their Spirit to God, their Skills in themselves, and their Service to nations.

Through years of ministry, these values crystallized into the G.O.D.S. acronym that guides everything we do. Each value represents a non-negotiable pillar in our mission to transform children into nation builders.

The 7 Decade Covenant

Our commitment to build a new Nigeria through god-children

In October 2022, KidsInspiring Nation entered into a sacred covenant with God - to dedicate 7 decades (70 years) to raising god-children who will transform Nigeria into the nation God destined it to be.

This covenant operates on the principle of "Every day, a decade" - meaning each day of ministry counts as a decade in the spirit. Through this, we're accelerating the transformation of our nation.

The 7 Decade Covenant focuses on raising leaders in every sector who will:

  • Govern with godly wisdom
  • Solve Nigeria's greatest challenges
  • Establish righteous systems
  • Be examples to other nations

How Our Programs Began

Daily Devotional

Born from a 2018 prayer meeting where children asked for daily guidance. Started with just 5 children, now reaches thousands daily through our app and print materials.

goDsCell

Began in 2019 when children requested smaller groups for deeper scripture study. The name was revealed during a powerful prayer session where God called them "gods to the gods of nations."

Psalm 119 Challenge

Inspired by a 12-year-old who memorized the entire chapter in 2018. The national competition launched in 2020 with just 3 schools, now involving over 200 schools nationwide.

goDs University

Started in 2021 after children completed the New Testament in goDsCell. The two-year program now has graduated over 500 children who've read the entire Bible.

Jesus Christ Concert

First held in 2018 as an alternative to secular Christmas shows. The concert has grown from 50 attendees to over 5,000, with children leading all aspects of production.

goDx Experience

Revealed in 2022 through Daniel 1:4-21 as a 5-stage discipleship model. Currently has 1,200 children progressing through the levels, with the first cohort reaching Level 3.
