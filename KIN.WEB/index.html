Our Story

KIDSINSPIRING began as a heart cry for the kingdom and our nation, Nigeria. Participating in the soul-winning drive of the Living Faith Church. In a conversation with the Spirit on soul-winning that is sustainable, the Spirit of God said: 'the soul of a child is as important as the soul of an adult.'

Thus, in 2017, we began to teach children after Sunday school at the church. What began as an after Sunday school event began to take shape into a Children Ministry unto the nations. These children were to be raised as gods from Daniel 1:4,8,17,20,21. In other words, a god to the gods of nations.

Today, we have a 5-7 year god-university program that raises children in three main ways - their Spirit to God, their Skills in themselves & their Service to Nations. Over the last five years, KidsInspiring has continued, witnessing children & teenagers, on a regular and consistent basis.

Psalm 119 Challenge

The Psalm 119 Challenge is an initiative of the KidsInspiring nation where children within the ages of 7 to 15 from primary and secondary schools across Nigeria learn Psalm 119 to compete for over One Million, One Hundred and Ninety One thousand, seven hundred and sixty Naira (1,191,760) in educational scholarships.

The god-University Model

Our Five Staged model as given by the Holy Spirit is a system to produce a god like Daniel of any child, in any nation, at a given stage of life.

1

Level 1 - Selection

Ps. 119 Challenge - memorise & recite all 176 verses of the longest chapter in God's WORD.

2

Level 2 - Spirit

The Bible Challenge - read & study all 1189 chapters of the bible.

3

Level 3 - Skills

The Biography Challenge - learn the stories of 100 unique men/women who have impacted the world in different fields.

4

Level 4 - Service

The 10,000 impact challenge - invest 10,000 hours using skills to serve the next generation and by that impact 1,000 young children.

5

Level 5 - Signs

The Daniel challenge - solve the most challenging problems in their fields, industries, calling, and nations.

3 Core Tripod System

  • Spirit

    It is our core, the foundation of everything we do at the KIN. The Spirit deals with praying the Holy Spirit, hearing the voice of God's Spirit, walking with and by the Spirit, and knowing the Spirit of Christ as a Friend.

  • Skills

    There is a gift God has deposited in every god-children. Something no school can teach and no one in the world can outcompete. That skill is the gifting for which they were created. Therefore, it is expected that each god develops their skills until they become icons to their world.

  • Service

    Every god is on a covenant to serve God in their generation or 7 decades. The primary way to serve God is using their Spirit through their skills to solve national & global challenges that would cause the world to look unto Jesus Christ, the Faith of the WORLD.
